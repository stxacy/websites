Our Story

More than a club —
a community

Surf Coast Cricket Club is a newly established, community-minded and inclusive club in the growing area of North Torquay. Based at the Banyul-Warri Fields Sporting Precinct on Merrijig Drive, we compete in the GCA.

Formed through the merger of the Surf Coast Stingrays and the Surf Coast Suns Football Netball Club, we field 4 senior men’s sides, a senior women’s team, and a growing junior program. We acknowledge the Wadawurrung People as the Traditional Custodians of the land on which we play.
