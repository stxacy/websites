03 — Our Philosophy
"A room is not a container.
It is a conversation
between light and life."

We believe design is inseparable from the people who inhabit it. Every commission begins with deep listening — understanding how a family moves through a morning, how a founder hosts a meeting, how a guest should feel when they first cross a threshold.

Material selections are made with patience. We build relationships with craftspeople whose hands leave traces in the work. The result is interiors that accumulate meaning over time rather than demand it immediately.

Ines Volm-Arnaud

04 — What We Do

Disciplines.

Residential Architecture

From single-family homes to penthouse renovations — we design dwellings that honour daily ritual and hold the weight of lived experience over generations.

Restaurants, hotels, retail flagships, and private clubs — spaces designed to define identity and shape the memory of every visitor who passes through them.

05 — The Studio

A small practice.
Deliberately so.

Volm Studio was founded in 2019 by Ines Volm-Arnaud in Amsterdam. We operate as a tight team of six — architects, an interior designer, and a material researcher. We take on fewer than twelve commissions a year to ensure that every project receives our full attention from brief to handover.

Our office is a converted textile warehouse in the Jordaan district. The building itself informs our practice: we believe in adaptive reuse, honest materiality, and the beauty of things that age with grace.
