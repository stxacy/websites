Who We Are

About Kingscote

Environmental haulage specialists operating within the Morrisroe Group — delivering responsible, efficient logistics for construction and industry.

Our Story

Built to Deliver

Kingscote Haulage was established to give the Morrisroe Group greater control over its transportation and environmental logistics operations. What began as an internal solution has grown into a specialist haulage business serving a broad external client base.

Operating from our base at Junction 2 of the M1 in Borehamwood, Hertfordshire, we are positioned to serve construction sites, industrial projects and environmental schemes across London and the South East.

Every movement we make is tracked against environmental performance standards. Our Euro VI compliant Scania tipper fleet represents our commitment to reducing the carbon footprint of construction logistics — without compromising on productivity.

Part of the Morrisroe Group

Kingscote Haulage sits within the Morrisroe Group — a leading construction and engineering business operating across the UK. This gives us access to group-level resources, expertise and procurement while maintaining the agility of a specialist contractor.
