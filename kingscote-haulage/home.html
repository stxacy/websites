Haulage & Muck Away

Kingscote Haulage removes waste materials from site. This includes moving soils and materials classified as hazardous, which may harm the environment or human health and require an extra duty of care. Clean inert waste, which does not come with such risks, as well as non-hazardous materials that may have low levels of contamination are also efficiently removed. The team carry out necessary testing to classify materials according to each site's requirements and can subsequently record and validate the removal of contaminated materials.

Recycled Aggregates

The team provide crushing and screening services to produce recycled aggregates for future use, grading them to specifications, such as 6f1, 6f5 and types 1 to 3, that are suitable for individual site requirements. This helps reduce the amount of waste being disposed of in landfills, cutting both costs and environmental impacts.

About Kingscote

Part of the Morrisroe Group

Kingscote Haulage was established to give the Morrisroe Group direct control over transportation and environmental haulage. Operating from Junction 2 of the M1 in Borehamwood, we serve both Group companies and a growing portfolio of external clients.

Our Euro VI compliant Scania tipper fleet and specialist operations team deliver a level of service — and environmental accountability — that sets us apart.
